Project: Cybersecurity news application

This project will be about developing an application to keep cyber security professionals informed about the latest threats and information, and news about the world of cyber security in general. There will also be in detail reviews about the newest antiviruses and malware protection, as well as a discussion forum about multiple subjects relating to security. This application can also be used by people with no particular knowledge of information technology or computers in general. As, security is an issue that should always be treated carefully, I believe this application can helpful to people and allow a peace of mind.

I believe this project can be interesting and useful, because of the growing cyber securities threats around the world. New viruses, trojans and malwares appear every day and people need to be alerted regularly about the new threats to their systems as well as their identities. It is reported that in Australia, businesses have seen an 18% raise in security attacks from 2017 to 2018 (Hudson, 2019). Therefore, I trust that this application can make things easier for a lot of people.

As stated before, this application has many uses, but its main purpose will be to provide users with the latest news reports and videos about new viruses, trojans and malwares, recent cyber-attacks and the newest technology to combat existing and future said attacks. After creating an account for the application there will be an easy to follow tutorial where it makes sure users don’t miss any of the information to better understand the application, as well as every other of its resources so it can be used to its fullest potential.

The app administrator will review and rate different anti viruses to help users identify their best needs, users will be able to create an account and customize the application the way they see fit, they will also have the option of receiving notifications so they won’t miss any recent content. Cyber security experts, as well as people in other fields can take advantage of updated information and the 24 hours newsfeed. Furthermore, users would be able to interact with each other, comment on stories and articles, easily share news stories in different social media formats and rate the information that will be provided to them, as well as give advice and opinions on how to make the app better for everyone. Users will also be able to save content offline and download videos for later use. The application will also be adjusted to tablet view and it will enable users to use new features to its advantages such as better video quality, as well as a more user-friendly look.

App collaborators will keep alert of the latest and most up to date advice to optimised user’s security, they will also work on guidelines for all levels of understanding, and information will be tailored to suit everyone’s needs. There will also be a store, so they can buy the newest protection as well (antiviruses, etc.), or other security software, promotional codes will also be awarded to new members for discounts at the store. Cyber security courses can also be researched and links will be provided to users with all the relevant information.

The MIT app inventor is one of the most well-known software development programs around, and is going to be our main source for creating this application. We will also be needing a windows pc in order to create our application, as well as an android device to test our app, we will also need to install an MIT inventor companion app on the android device (MIT app inventor, 2019). Both our windows pc and android device will have to be connected to the same WIFI network for this method to work.

For the creation of this application, we will have to possess a variety of skills including development, writing, programming and design. Furthermore, as mentioned before, for hardware equipment, we will be needing a windows pc as well as an android device. The development of this app could be relatively simple and straight forward with help of MIT inventor, nonetheless developers will have to keep on top of their game to fix any problems that may arise. We believe that all the materials that will be used can be acquired fairly easily, as for the skills we will have to develop our knowledge of information technology and computer science contribute to the possibility of completing this project.
